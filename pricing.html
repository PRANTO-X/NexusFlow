Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and plans

What's included in the free plan?

The free Developer plan includes 1,000 quantum transactions per month, basic quantum encryption, access to our sandbox environment, community support, and all core APIs. It's perfect for testing, prototyping, and small projects.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to the new features. When downgrading, the changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. All payments are processed securely with quantum encryption.

Is there a discount for annual billing?

Yes! When you choose annual billing, you'll save 20% compared to monthly billing. This applies to all paid plans and is automatically calculated when you select annual billing.

What happens if I exceed my transaction limit?

If you exceed your monthly transaction limit, additional transactions will be billed at your plan's overage rate. For the Growth plan, this is $0.015 per additional transaction. We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit.
